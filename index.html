What is Stress.tw?

Stress.tw is a free online stress relief tool that helps you quickly relieve anxiety and stress from work and daily life through interactive physics simulation.

Core Features

  • Interactive Creation: Click screen to create colorful bouncy balls
  • Real Physics: Realistic simulation powered by Matter.js physics engine
  • Explosive Effects: Cascade explosion effects provide ultimate stress relief experience
  • Sound Feedback: Carefully designed sound effects enhance immersion

Use Cases

Perfect for work break relaxation, study break stress relief, bedtime relaxation, and emotional regulation. Works on mobile and desktop browsers with no download required.

Technical Highlights

Built with GSAP animation library, Matter.js physics engine, and Howler.js audio system to ensure smooth 60fps experience and realistic physical interactions.
